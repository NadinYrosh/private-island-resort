Travel Arrangements

Many guests make their own travel arrangements, booking airlines, hotels and resorts on the internet. Your stay at Oasis can be booked on this website or you can email us direct for availability and specials. Your booking with us includes complimentary return transfers from the airport to the island and are co-ordinated with your booking.
