About Oasis Island Resort

Oasis Island Resort is located on St. Johns, U.S. Virgin Islands. This beautiful boutique hotel accomodates up to seventeen people and is the perfect choice for your private island getaway. With has several luxurious pools, nine natural mineral water hot springs, a world class spa featuring celebrity messuses, and three private waterfall villas to soothe your soul. The resort also provides great activities on the island or in the water. Oasis Island is the perfect size for your private retreat.
