Activities

  • Lounge in a giant SandBox. Unwind in our new beach lounge, The SandBox, located at Guy Harvey Outpost. Enjoy your own piece of paradise with VIP Cabanas, signature drinks, dining options and unforgettable sunsets around the fire pit.
  • Fly high on JetLev. Take your beach vacation to new heights at TradeWinds and experience JetLev. You can literally fly over the Gulf and soar up to 30 feet in the air.
